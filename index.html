FAQs

What is Belto Doc?

Belto Doc is an AI-powered platform designed for academic environments. It helps maintain academic integrity by allowing professors to set submission guidelines while providing students with a smart, secure, and collaborative workspace for their assignments.

How does Belto Doc help prevent cheating?

Belto Doc helps prevent cheating by creating a controlled environment where professors can set restrictions. For example, they can disable content pasting from third-party websites or track if a student navigates away from the screen.

Can Belto Doc be integrated with our current Learning Management System (LMS)?

Yes, Belto Doc integrates with major LMS platforms like Canvas.

What kind of AI training does Belto Doc use?

Belto Doc creates individual chat sessions for each class and lecture. Each lecture has its own dedicated chat, trained exclusively on your course content. This ensures students study using your materials without distractions from outside sources.

Is there support for group projects and collaborative work?

Absolutely. Professors can enable collaboration on submissions, allowing groups of students to work together on a single project.
